Welcome to Computer Studies Department!

Here in Computer Studies Department, we offer different courses regarding both scientific and technological studies which is relevant in seeking excellence in academic programs and strive to produce Christian p rofessionals who can meet the manpower needs of the industries in the fast developing CALABARZON area. CSD fosters Information Technology and Theoretical Computer Science through relevant applications and research. Guided by the Lasallian values of Faith, Zeal, and Communion, it aims to develop well-rounded students who possess the five C's of a true Lasallian: Committed, Confident, Compassionate, Competent, and Christian. CSD aims to provide students with both professionalism and Christianity at the same which may be carried out in their future jobs and in their individual lives.
